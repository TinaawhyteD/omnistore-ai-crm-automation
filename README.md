What the system does

This system simulates an e-commerce CRM that:

* Collects customer interest data through a simple form
* Uses AI to analyze customer intent and categorize buyers
* Assigns an intent score (0–100) based on purchase likelihood
* Automatically classifies customers as Hot, Warm, or Cold
* Stores all leads in a structured CRM (Google Sheets)
* Sends automated email responses based on customer intent
* Triggers follow-up actions for potential buyers

🎯 How it is helpful

This system helps e-commerce businesses:

* Identify high-value customers faster
* Reduce lost sales from untracked leads
* Automate customer follow-ups without manual effort
* Improve conversion rates through prioritization
* Save time by removing manual lead sorting
* Ensure consistent communication with potential buyers

Overall, it acts as an AI sales assistant that helps turn customer interest into actual purchases.

🧰 Tools used

* n8n (workflow automation)
* Google Sheets (CRM database)
* OpenAI / AI model (customer intent analysis)
* Google Forms (lead collection)
* Email (Gmail or SMTP for automated responses)
